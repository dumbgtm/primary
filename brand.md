# dumbGTM Brand Identity

_Revised: palette and logo direction provided by Ishan; design updated to match._

## Positioning
GTM advice that isn't trying to sell you a $20k retainer. Contrarian, anti-establishment,
calls out slop and overdone tactics (AI SDR spam, "10x your pipeline" LinkedIn grifters,
frameworks-for-the-sake-of-frameworks). Tone: whimsical, funny, a little silly — but the
takes underneath are sharp. Think: the smartest person at the party who refuses to talk
like it.

## Name treatment
"dumb gtm" — both lowercase, single weight, sits as one wordmark (not split-styled). The
joke: everyone else is overcomplicating go-to-market with jargon and 40-slide frameworks.
This is the dumb, obvious version that actually works. Written as "dumbGTM" in running
prose/sentences, but the logo and page titles use the lowercase "dumb gtm" wordmark.

## Voice
- Say the thing everyone's thinking but won't post.
- - Short sentences. Punchlines land harder short.
  - - Roast the tactic, not the person doing it (mostly).
    - - Never use the phrase "unlock growth" unironically.
      - - Confident, not cruel. Silly, not sloppy.
       
        - ## Color palette
        - - Paper (background): `#E9FFF9` — "Frozen Water," a near-white mint. Cooler and cleaner than a stark white.
          - - Ink (text): `#1D3354` — "Twilight Indigo." Primary text color, replaces black — softer, still high contrast on mint.
            - - Siren (primary accent): `#D64045` — "Scarlet Rush." CTAs, buttons, active nav state, key highlights.
              - - Frost (secondary accent): `#9ED8DB` — "Frosted Blue." Tag backgrounds, card hover states, soft highlight fills.
                - - Cerulean (tertiary accent): `#467599` — "Rich Cerulean." Body links, secondary emphasis.
                  - - Slate (muted): `#5B6B85` — muted blue-gray derived from the palette, for captions/metadata/secondary text.
                   
                    - Deliberately not used as a solid background or text color: Twilight Indigo is reserved for
                    - text and dark section blocks (like the "why we exist" band on the homepage), not the page
                    - background — mint stays the base so the site reads light and clean.
                   
                    - ## Typography
                    - - Display / headlines: **Inter Tight**, bold/extrabold (700-800) — condensed, confident,
                      -   same family as body copy so headlines and paragraphs feel like one voice, not two fonts
                      -     fighting each other.
                      - - Body: **Inter** — clean, readable, gets out of the way so the jokes land.
                       
                        - (Previous version of this brand used Archivo Black + Permanent Marker for a "zine" look —
                        - replaced per design direction below.)
                       
                        - ## Logo concept
                        - Wordmark only: "dumb gtm", all lowercase, single weight (Inter Tight ExtraBold), single
                        - color (Twilight Indigo for normal use). No stamp, no underline bar, no split styling — the
                        - mark is confident and plain on purpose.
                       
                        - - `logo.svg` — horizontal wordmark, Twilight Indigo on transparent background.
                          - - `logo-stamp.svg` — square mark for favicon/social: Twilight Indigo rounded-square
                            -   background, a lowercase "d" in Frozen Water, with a small Scarlet Rush dot as a wink of
                            -     color (the one bit of "look twice" whimsy allowed in an otherwise minimal mark).
                           
                            - ## Design texture — clean and minimal
                            - Moved away from the earlier "punk zine" direction (dashed borders, stamped rotated tags,
                            - marker-highlighter styling) to match the polished, restrained feel of the wordmark:
                           
                            - - Cards and containers: soft rounded corners (`rounded-xl`/`rounded-2xl`), thin low-opacity
                              -   borders (`border-ink/10`) instead of thick 2px borders.
                              -   - No rotation effects, no dashed borders, no "stamp" treatment anywhere.
                                  - - Tags/labels: soft rounded pills in Frost at low opacity, not stamped/rotated boxes.
                                    - - Buttons: rounded-lg, solid Siren fill for primary actions, subtle bordered/ghost style
                                      -   for secondary actions. Hover states are color/opacity shifts, not rotation.
                                      -   - Still bold and contrarian in voice/copy — the restraint is visual, not tonal.
